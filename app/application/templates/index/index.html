What do we offer?

Dealroom

In line with the 2022 theme and considering the global pandemic, the AYAF DealRoom will be held virtually. The Bank, through the 2022 Virtual Investor DealRoom, aims to create access to capital and markets which are often the two main hurdles for youth agripreneurs to overcome when scaling up their businesses

Mentorship

Following the training and bootcamp, the participating agripreneurs will receive follow-up mentorship and coaching (virtually) for a period of nine months to ensure that the competition funds/awards are utilised as agreed by winning businesses and to track the overall progress of all 25 virtually. The mentorship activity will also link the AgriPitch participants to relevant resources throughout the nine-month period and beyond.
