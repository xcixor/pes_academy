TOMAVè AgriTech
Company overview

Designing digital solutions for agriculture, producing and marketing agricultural products, setting up projects and providing services.

Mvutu
Company overview

Mvutu is a Congolese platform that connects farmers and vendors to ensure a fair and reliable food supply network. Leveraging its network and extensive industry experience, Mvutu buys from farmers and suppliers at fair market prices and ships the produce to thousands of street vendors in the cities of Congo and Kinshasa. To further strengthen the ecosystem, Mvutu offers microloans and technical assistance to farmers, distributors.

FASO ELEVAGE
Company overview

Faso Elevage is a limited liability company (SARL) created by Burkinabé engineers in animal production. The primary objective is to modernize and professionalize the agricultural sector in Burkina Faso and Africa, promote local breeding materials and promote organic breeding.

Bilanga Solutions
Company overview

Bilanga is a digital platform allowing small farmers to have access to markets and to better sell their products, from organic and sustainable agriculture, to agri-food companies and urban consumers who benefit from the best prices and a transparent procurement process. Our end-to-end process connects farmers to commercial fresh produce retailers in Kinshasa and across the DR Congo. The BILANGA platform facilitates transactions and protects payments on behalf of farmers, resulting in increased transparency and improved cash flow.

AfriCereal Group
Company overview

AfriCereal Group is a technology company that offers mechanization of agricultural operations, bringing together players in the value chains and management of agricultural equipment and infrastructure. Through its services, the company simplifies agricultural operations, contributes to the reduction of post-harvest losses and improves the production and income of small producers. Thus, small producers have access to affordable agricultural machinery to increase their productivity.

Duketrah Fisheries
Company overview

Duketrah Fisheries is an Agribusiness that is into fish production and value addition (through processing and packaging). With the goal of providing safe, healthy and nutritious fish products to a large population of people, we sustainably produce and process fish using better aquaculture practices and improved processing methods as well as packaging materials which are of reduced hazard to the consumer and processors health as well as the safety of the environment.

AMRA SUARL ( vet info)
Company overview

VET INFO GROUP is a start-up specialized in agro-business. We support breeders to produce healthy milk, meat and eggs. We transform livestock waste into agricultural biofertilizer. VET INFO GROUP is the first Agro-business market place in West Africa.

Farmer Lifeline Technologies
Company overview

Farmer LifeLine Technologies is an Kenya startup that helps farmers to get ahead of pests and pathogens with a proprietary disease detection device.

Contanna
Company overview

Contanna is a fair trade tea bar chain that promotes African roots by offering hot and iced drinks made from plantations from our lands.

Minagro Group
Company overview

Minagro supports agribusinesses by proposing greener solutions to improve the global impact of the agricultural sector. Minagro develops innovative products and solutions for crop care applications based on sustainable raw materials leveraging the strengths of the MINAFIN Group in green and fine chemicals.
Minagro is a supplier of ingredients and innovative solutions with a specific focus on agricultural input markets. We develop products and provide application support.

FulltechCongo
Company overview

FulltechCongo, Full Technologies is a Congolese company specialized in:

1. Electrical installation and repair
2. Renewable energy :
* Installation of photovoltaic solar panels
* Design of solar energy storage and optimization systems
3. Planning and maintenance of green spaces
4. Car & property cleaning
* Fixed car cleaning (our station)
* Mobile car cleaning (at your home)
* Cleaning building, public spaces, stained glass, pavement, tiles.
ChemChemAgro
Company overview

ChemChemAgro has as its activity:
manufacturing of hives, aid for the harvesting and raising of bees (already operational), production, processing, marketing of honey and honey by-products (in the development yard).

ChemChemAgro also trains young people and farmers in beekeeping.
Product: High quality table honey and honey jams.

World Jolly Generation Engineering and Trading ( WJGET SARL)
Company overview

The World Jolly Generation Engineering and Trading Society trains in the field of renewable energy.
Three months theoretical and practical training of photovoltaic technician and 3 months practical internship in the company and partner companies in the field.
It also gives opportunity to prepare for ECOWA certification and get recognized certification in all 16 ECOWA countries.

Agrisiti (Maatalous Nasah)
Company overview

Maatalous Nasah is an agritech startup based in Nigeria focused on promoting urban Agriculture and facilitating Agri-Marketing using digital technology. We achieve this via our Agri-Marketing Arm called Farmisphere, which is a web-based application to provide farmers with timely market access.

Eco-Protein
Company overview

Eco-Protein is an insect technology startup that transforms organic food waste into high value insect protein for the animal feed industry by using the black soldier fly insect as a recycling agent.

Sediba
Company overview

Sediba is a virtual farmers' market that connects buyers, small holder and emerging farmers to a reliable and fair market .

Mgom'mera Seed investments
Company overview

Mgom’mera produces drought tolerant, high yielding and nutrient dense varieties of maize and legumes.
As the Malawian agricultural sector seeks to combat effects of climate change and drought, Mgom’mera aims to tackle this issue from seed technology and modern innovations in irrigation farming.

MiAgro Shoppers Limited
Company overview

MiAgro Shoppers enterprise is an e-commerce digital shopping platform based in Kampala, Uganda, with supporting electronnic tools (Android application 'MiAgro App' on Playstore/Applestore and e-commerce website 'MiAgro Shoppers') offering a range of products from agricultural groceries, gas accessories, supermarket items, wines & spirits & kitchenware delivered to your doorstep.

Legendary Foods Ltd
Company overview

We are a food tech business in Ghana on a mission to produce West & Central Africa's most cost-effective, nutritious, resource-efficient and accessible form of protein, palm larvae, with technology and farming systems proudly built in Ghana.

Solaristique Nigeria Limited
Company overview

Solaristique Nigeria is a recycling company that is focused on making sustainable clean energy and environment-friendly solutions targeted at BoP households, clinics, and farmers in rural and peri-urban communities to SMEs , Hospitals, Big corporations and Households in urban areas. We aim to make the greatest possible global impact in addressing the environmental issues associated wastes.
Our Core sectors are WASTE COLLECTION, COLD STORAGE, and ALTERNATIVE BUILDING MATERIALS.

Osomobegbe Global Ventures Limited
Company overview

Osomobegbe Global Ventures Limited is a company that is into sales of solar dryers and rendering of solar drying services to smallholder farmers of eprishable vegetable products so as to enable them mitigate post harvest losses on their products. We are also into women empowerment in entrepreneurship and agriculture. We are into biofuel production and solar PV systems installation across Nigeria

TIC Agrobusiness
Company overview

We offer digital solutions to facilitate the sharing of knowledge and information at the producer level.
We provide Agricultural Advice Using Agricultural Messaging Application

Farmhut Africa Private Limited
Company overview

Farmhut shortens the agro supply value chan by aggregating markets, connecting farmers, restaurants, retailers and vendors together. The food-system is improved due to improved operations of smallholder farmers in the form of lower post-harvest losses, informed decisions, and greater farmer resilience to climate and financial shocks.

Etoduma SARL
Company overview

Etoduma SARL is an agri-food company specialises in the processing of plantain, sweet potato into chips. It also ensures the distribution of these chips in the shelves of supermarkets and large partner shops, university campuses primary and secondary schools and bus stations.

FarmWallet Limited
Company overview

FarmWallet has a system that strengthens the weak linkages among agri-value chain players, by linking and facilitating transactions among them; farmers can access inputs, information, and markets.
